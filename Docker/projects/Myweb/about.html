About Us

LBEF Healthcare Publishing

LBEF Healthcare Publications is solely dedicated to knowledge, trustworthy content, and insights that facilitate the empirical engagement of healthcare workers and the masses. Publications include a wide array of materials on the latest research findings in medicine down through very helpful tips for everyday health. With a commitment to accuracy and relevance, we would like to connect with, inform, and educate our readers on various relevant healthcare-related issues, building a community wherein knowledge and understanding will promote better health. Be it a professional working in healthcare and looking for the latest knowledge or any person interested in learning about his options for improving health, LBEF Healthcare Publications is your trusted resource of information you can rely on.

What do we offer?

    Up-to-date in-depth analyses and trends on medical research, new treatments, and health care.

    Articles by health professionals; most are experts in their field.

    Actionable advice to help readers maintain or improve their personal health and well-being.

    Strategies and information on how to prevent common diseases through lifestyle changes and early detection.

    Patient-empowerment tools and resources providing knowledge about health conditions and treatment options.

    Information on the latest breakthroughs in medicine and health care technology.

    Holistic health information covering mental wellness, nutrition, and fitness.

    Facilitation of a positive alliance among patients through commenting, forums, and discussions.

Our editorial process

Detailed sessions of ideation and planning result in LBEF Healthcare Publications' editorial process. A rapport with key points of interest is maintained, mainly relevant to the audience: healthcare professionals and secondarily the lay public. Our editorial team goes out to work on the ground to understand what new trends in healthcare, medical advancements, or areas simply requiring trustworthy information are crucial. Ideas are further refined through editorial priorities, reader feedback, and expertise within our team.

From here, our healthcare writers and researchers dive into full development. We tap into very credible sources: peer-reviewed journals, medical textbooks, and interviews with experts to get accurate, up-to-date information. The content focuses on making the information clear and accessible to the public. Our writers extract these very complicated medical concepts into easily understandable gems of information, ensuring that the content is informative yet easy for our very varied audience to comprehend.

Writing at LBEF Healthcare Publications is collaborative. It involves skilled writers and editors who set high standards in posts. Drafting the articles is done by the writers, after which they are reviewed for accuracy, tone, and adherence to our editorial guidelines. The editors then provide constructive feedback on refining narrative flow and clarity of content. Grammar, style, and formatting are meticulously checked to aid readability and engagement. This iterative process ensures that each article makes it through our quality benchmarks before moving on to the next step.

Before publication, all content goes through a rigorous review. Thorough fact-checking of medical information is undertaken regarding the accuracy of the information provided and the backing of claims with credible sources. Some articles may also be medically reviewed by licensed professionals or, in some cases, subject matter experts, who oversee medical accuracy and clarity. Articles are then queued for publishing in accordance with our editorial calendar. We also gather reader feedback to help determine what's working and what's not using our engagement metrics. Such a feedback loop not only puts us at a position where the editorial strategy of LBEF Healthcare Publications is continuing to progress but also creates a credible source of information while upholding quality healthcare.
