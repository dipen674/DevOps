Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to some of the most common questions about our organization and our initiatives.

General Questions

  • What is LBEF Healthcare?

    LBEF Healthcare is dedicated to providing comprehensive health and wellness services to students, faculty, and staff at LBEF campus.

  • Where is LBEF Healthcare located?

    We are located on the ground floor of the main campus building. You can find detailed directions on our Contact Us page.

  • What services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of services including medical consultations, mental health support, nutrition counseling, and wellness programs.

  • Are your services confidential?

    Yes, all our services are confidential. We adhere to strict privacy policies to ensure your information is secure.

Appointments

  • How do I make an appointment?

    You can make an appointment by calling our front desk at (123) 456-7890 or by using the online booking system on our website.

  • Do I need an appointment to see a healthcare provider?

    While we accept walk-ins for urgent issues, we recommend making an appointment to ensure you receive timely care.

  • What should I bring to my appointment?

    Please bring your student ID, any relevant medical records, and a list of any medications you are currently taking.

Insurance and Payments

  • Do you accept insurance?

    Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please check with our front desk for the most up-to-date list of accepted insurers.

  • What if I don’t have insurance?

    If you don’t have insurance, you can still receive care at our facility. We offer affordable rates and payment plans to accommodate all students.

  • How can I pay for services?

    We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. Payment is due at the time of service unless other arrangements have been made.

Mental Health Services

  • What mental health services do you provide?

    We offer individual counseling, group therapy, crisis intervention, and psychiatric consultations.

  • How do I access mental health services?

    You can access our mental health services by making an appointment with one of our counselors through our website or by calling our office.

  • Is there a cost for mental health services?

    Most of our mental health services are provided at no cost to students. Some specialized services may have a fee, which will be discussed with you beforehand.

Health and Wellness Programs

  • What kind of wellness programs do you offer?

    We offer a variety of wellness programs including fitness classes, nutrition workshops, stress management seminars, and wellness challenges.

  • How can I participate in wellness programs?

    You can sign up for our wellness programs through our website or by visiting our office. Stay updated on upcoming programs by checking our Events page.
