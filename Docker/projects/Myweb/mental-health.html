Mental Health

Counseling Sessions

One-on-one counseling with trained professionals to address personal concerns.

See more...

Our counseling sessions provide a safe and confidential environment for students to discuss their concerns. Our trained professionals offer support for various issues, including anxiety, depression, stress management, and personal growth.

Stress Management Workshops

Workshops designed to teach effective stress coping strategies.

See more...

Our stress management workshops equip students with practical tools to handle academic and personal stress. Learn techniques such as mindfulness, time management, and cognitive restructuring to improve your overall well-being.

Peer Support Groups

Safe spaces for students to connect, share experiences, and support each other.

See more...

Our peer support groups foster a sense of community and understanding among students. These groups provide a platform to share experiences, learn from others, and develop a support network to navigate the challenges of student life.
