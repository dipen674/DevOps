Available Care

General Medical Care

Routine check-ups, treatment for common illnesses, and management of chronic conditions.

Mental Health Services

Counseling, therapy sessions, and support groups to help students manage stress, anxiety, and other mental health concerns.

Women's Health

Gynecological exams, Female Health Care Advices, and more.

Men's Health

Health screenings, sexual health consultations, and treatment for male-specific conditions.

Immunizations

Vaccinations for flu, HPV, meningitis, and other preventable diseases.

Laboratory Services

Blood tests, urinalysis, and other diagnostic tests.

Pharmacy Services

On-site pharmacy for prescription medications and over-the-counter drugs.

Health Education

Workshops and resources on nutrition, exercise, sexual health, and substance abuse prevention.

Emergency Care

First aid and emergency response for urgent situations.

Telehealth Services

Virtual consultations with healthcare providers for convenience and accessibility.

Nutrition Counseling

Personalized dietary advice and meal planning to support healthy eating habits.

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation services to help students recover from injuries and improve physical function.
