Get Involved

Join our community and make a difference! There are many ways you can get involved with LBEF and support health and wellness on campus.

Volunteer Opportunities

We have a variety of volunteer opportunities available. Whether you want to help with events, support our programs, or provide administrative assistance, we have a role for you!

  • Event Volunteers

    Assist with planning, setting up, and running health and wellness events.

    As an event volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to:

    • Coordinate with event organizers
    • Set up event spaces
    • Assist attendees and answer questions
    • Help with post-event cleanup

  • Program Support

    Help us with our ongoing health programs and initiatives.

    As a program support volunteer, you'll:

    • Assist in implementing health programs
    • Provide support to program participants
    • Help collect and analyze program data
    • Contribute to program improvement efforts

  • Administrative Assistance

    Provide valuable support in our office with various tasks.

    As an administrative assistant, you'll help with:

    • Data entry and organization
    • Answering phone calls and emails
    • Assisting with scheduling and appointments
    • General office management tasks

Internship Programs

Gain valuable experience and contribute to our mission by participating in one of our internship programs. Opportunities are available in various areas including public health, wellness education, and more.

  • Public Health Internship

    Work on projects related to public health and wellness.

    As a public health intern, you'll:

    • Assist in conducting health surveys
    • Participate in community health initiatives
    • Help analyze health data
    • Contribute to health education materials

  • Wellness Education Internship

    Assist with developing and delivering wellness education programs.

    As a wellness education intern, you'll:

    • Help design wellness workshops
    • Assist in creating educational materials
    • Support wellness events on campus
    • Contribute to social media wellness campaigns

Donate

Your contributions make a significant impact. Support our programs and help us promote health and wellness in our community.

  • Monetary Donations

    Your financial support helps us fund our health and wellness programs.

    Your monetary donations help us:

    • Fund health education programs
    • Provide wellness resources to students
    • Organize health awareness events
    • Support ongoing wellness initiatives

  • In-Kind Donations

    Donate goods or services that can benefit our programs and events.

    We welcome in-kind donations such as:

    • Health and wellness books
    • Fitness equipment
    • Healthy snacks for events
    • Professional services (e.g., wellness coaching)
