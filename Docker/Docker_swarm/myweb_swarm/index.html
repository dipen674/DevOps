Welcome to the LBEF Health and Wellness Club

This is a student organization dedicated to promoting healthy habits and wellness on campus. Our mission is to provide resources, events, and support for students to lead a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

"He who has health, has hope; and he who has hope, has everything." - Arabian Proverb

Our Initiatives

  • Physical Health: Encouraging regular physical activity, fitness classes, and sports.
  • Spiritual Health: Offering mindfulness and meditation sessions, and spiritual growth resources.
  • Nutrition: Providing nutritional advice, healthy eating workshops, and cooking classes.
  • Mental Health: Supporting mental well-being through counseling, stress management workshops, and peer support groups.
  • Wellness Tips: Sharing practical tips for maintaining overall wellness and balance in student life.
  • Available Care: Informing students about healthcare services available on campus.
  • Healthy Decisions: Promoting informed and healthy lifestyle choices through educational campaigns.
